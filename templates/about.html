Why HANSEL

HANSEL is a friend of GRETIL. Since GRETIL no longer has support to grow, we need a new place to accommodate new Sanskrit e-text material. HANSEL aims to be open and easy for students and scholars to contribute to. Contributed material may be upgraded forms of e-texts from previous repositories like GRETIL or SARIT, or it may be entirely new. Let's work together to expand the amount of high-quality Sanskrit material online!

"Human-accessible" means that material will always exist in forms that people can use directly, such as plain-text and HTML.

"NLP-ready" means that the data will also be structured in anticipation of computational applications, for those with natural-language processing ideas they'd like to try out on as much data as possible.

In addition to these two core values, HANSEL will also maintain a high standard of transparency, with clearly documented scholarly sources, open-source and versioned data and code, and easily searchable metadata.
