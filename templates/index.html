Contributing

If you have material you'd like to add, whatever its current form (e.g., Word doc, plain text, XML, or just PDF), get in contact with whatever you have. The curatorial team — currently a one-man show, but hopefully more soon! — will help with continued processing and formatting. Technical expertise welcome but not required! And of course, modifying existing material in the collection is also encouraged.

Contact

For inquiries or contributions, please email tyler.g.neill (Gmail).
